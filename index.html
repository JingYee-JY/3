Here They Come!

Koeh Sia Yong

Here They Come! was painted by artist Koeh Sia Yong in 1965. The gestures and expressions of the people depicted in the painting, combined with the work's title, suggest that it captures the moment when they spotted the authorities and are packing up their wares to escape trouble.
